A Test Behind A Show

SOMA, a captivating installation at Berlin's Hamburger Bahnhof, presents Belgian artist Carsten Höller's intricate work, engaging all senses with canary melodies and reindeer fragrance. Born in Brussels in 1961, Höller created his most sophisticated piece, featuring twelve reindeer, twenty-four canaries, eight mice, and two flies as living protagonists, offering a multisensory exploration at the intersection of art, nature, and sensory experiences.
